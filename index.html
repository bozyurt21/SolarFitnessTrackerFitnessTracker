About Our Project
Our project is with using solar energy as an input source, wirelessly charging our own fitness tracker. We used transmitter and reciever coil design for transfering energy wirelessly. In our fitness tracker with accelerometer sensor (ADXL345), tempreature sensor (SH---) and heart-reate sensor (MAX30102) give user capability of counting their steps, temperature of the outdoor and heart-beat count and oxygen level reading using Arduino nano every. The most challenging part of our project was designing and testing transmitter circuit due to energy efficiency problem comes with using both wireless energy transfer and solar panel. Throughout fall semester though we learned and improved our project in a good way. Hope you all enjoyed our project.
